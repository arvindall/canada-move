# California DMV → ICBC Driver's License Swap Checklist
Use this with `../checklist.md` before and after arrival.

## Before leaving California
- Request California DMV proof of your original licensing date
- Request a driver record and, if needed, a First Issue Date Letter / letter of experience
- Keep old expired California licenses if they show an earlier issue date than the current card
- Save copies of everything you receive from the DMV

## What to bring to ICBC
- Current California driver's license
- California DMV proof of original issue date / driving experience
- Passport or other accepted identity document
- Proof of BC address

## Timing
- Apply within 90 days of becoming a BC resident

## Important rule
- If you cannot prove at least 2 years of non-learner driving experience, ICBC can place you into the Graduated Licensing Program (GLP)

## Insurance experience follow-up
- ICBC may request additional California driving-history / insurance-history proof later if you want to claim up to 15 years of insurance experience credit

## Do not assume
- Do not assume the current California record automatically shows the original issue date ICBC wants
- Do not assume ICBC only needs the current license card
- Do not assume unsupported DMV handling details such as special sealing or in-person certification unless the DMV or ICBC specifically requires them
