# Mover Quote Request Template
Copy, customize, and send this to each mover in your initial quote round.

## Subject
Quote request — downsized California to Burnaby BC household move

## Email / message
Hello [Company / Rep Name],

I am requesting a quote for a cross-border household move from [pickup city, California] to Burnaby, British Columbia.

This is a downsized move. My current expected shipment is:

- mostly boxes
- a few small furniture pieces
- maybe one bed / mattress
- not a full furniture-heavy 2-bedroom load

I need a full-service move rather than a self-pack / self-load option.

## Services I want quoted
- full packing at origin
- loading at origin
- cross-border transport from California to Burnaby
- inside delivery into the apartment in Burnaby
- unloading at destination
- [optional] unpacking
- [optional] debris removal
- [optional] bed disassembly / reassembly if one bed is included

## Timing
- target pickup window: [date range]
- target delivery window: [date range]
- if your schedule differs, please state your realistic pickup and delivery windows

## Please confirm in writing
1. Whether this quote is binding, not-to-exceed, or estimate-only
2. What weight / volume assumptions the quote is based on
3. Any minimum shipment size or minimum charge for a small move
4. Whether your company directly handles pickup and delivery, or whether any leg is brokered / subcontracted
5. Whether you regularly handle California → British Columbia household moves
6. How you coordinate CBSA customs clearance for settler's effects / goods to follow
7. Whether inside delivery is included
8. Whether unpacking, debris removal, and bed setup are included or extra
9. Whether you can provide a certificate of insurance for a Burnaby apartment building if required
10. Any excluded items or categories I should plan to keep out of the shipment
11. Exactly what would trigger a change order or repricing after the quote is issued

## Helpful context
- I am building my BSF186 / BSF186A goods-to-follow spreadsheet now and can share a rough inventory
- I can also send photos / video of the remaining shipment
- If relevant, I can share building move-in rules such as elevator reservation windows and COI requirements

Please send:

- your written quote
- the quote type (binding / not-to-exceed / estimate)
- your weight / volume assumptions
- your delivery window assumptions
- your valuation / liability coverage options

Thank you,

[Your full name]
[Phone number]
[Email]
[Pickup city, CA]
[Destination neighborhood or Burnaby building area, if helpful]

## Suggested attachments
- rough box count and large-item list
- a few photos or a short walkthrough video
- draft BSF186A spreadsheet excerpt
- building move-in rules if already available
